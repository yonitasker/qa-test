Hello, my name is Yoni Tasker.

I have been a tour guide in Israel for over ten years.



Over the years, I have led many participants of all ages.



The participants experienced a deep and meaningful journey with themselves

by travelling the diverse environment that Israel,

a small country by the definition of most people,

has to offer those who choose to explore its landscapes.



In recent years, I have developed an expertise
in guiding Birthright groups, also known as Taglit,
on a ten-day journey around Israel.

During this time the participants discover a rich inner and outer world
through experiences, thoughts and interpersonal acquaintances
that help them develop as human beings in the modern world.

It is a great privilege for me
to accompany this process through the arrival of
the cities, viewpoints, nature hikes
and many other magical places.
